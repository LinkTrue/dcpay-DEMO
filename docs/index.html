Pain

What problem DCPay solves

  • Single-provider risk when one RPC is wrong, delayed, or unavailable.
  • Conflicting status when different providers report different outcomes.
  • Manual reconciliation load on ops, support, and compliance teams.
How

How DCPay works

  • Checks the same payment across multiple RPC providers.
  • When stakes are high and results differ, runs consensus on the correct state.
  • Returns one trusted outcome via API/webhooks, with audit-ready logs.
Fit

Who can use it

  • Payment processors, exchanges, and fintech infrastructure teams.
  • E-commerce and SaaS businesses accepting crypto directly.
  • DAO and treasury operators that need reliable settlement records.
  • Audit and compliance teams validating on-chain outcomes.
